UlarMark

Mark Up Screenshots in Seconds

A fast, native macOS app for annotating screenshots and images. Arrows, text, blur, callouts, and more — then copy or save without breaking your flow.

Local-First Privacy: Your images never leave your device.

A Complete Markup Toolkit

Everything you need to explain an image clearly.

Numbered Steps & Callouts

Number every step with badges in circles or squares — decimal, alphabetical, or Roman — that renumber themselves automatically. Add speech and thought bubbles in a dozen styles, from clean ovals to comic-book explosions.

Shapes & Arrows for Every Point

Straight or curved arrows with a choice of heads, boxes, ellipses, and lines — each in solid, dashed, or dotted strokes. Sweep a highlighter, underline, or scribble freehand to draw the eye exactly where you want it.

Style Every Detail

Every tool opens a full property panel — border thickness, color, and style, fill and shadow, corner roundness, bubble shape and tail. Tweak anything and watch it update live on the canvas.

Build Your Own Tools

Save any tool with its exact style as a reusable custom tool, star the ones you use most, and grab them instantly from the sidebar — your markup kit, set up your way.

Common Questions

Where are my images stored? Everything stays on your device. UlarMark only opens images you choose and only writes files you explicitly save or export. Nothing is uploaded. Is it a subscription? No. UlarMark is a one-time purchase on the Mac App Store — buy it once and own it, with no subscription and no in-app purchases. What can I open? Paste from the clipboard or open PNG, JPEG, TIFF, GIF, HEIC, WebP, BMP, and more — via the Open panel, Finder "Open With", or drag-and-drop. Does it speak my language? UlarMark ships with 18 interface languages and picks yours automatically from your system settings.
